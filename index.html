Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Gaucho Plugins.

How do I install a Gaucho Plugins plugin?
Download the plugin from WordPress.org or from your Gaucho Plugins account, then in your WordPress dashboard go to Plugins → Add New → Upload Plugin, install and activate it, and enter your license key to unlock the premium features.
Do your plugins work with WooCommerce and Stripe?
Yes. Payment Page, Split Pay, and China Payments connect directly to Stripe, and several of our plugins extend WooCommerce — including Stripe Connect split payments, Alipay/WeChat/UnionPay checkout, and product buyback offers.
Is there a free version of each plugin?
Most Gaucho Plugins have a free version on WordPress.org plus a premium upgrade that adds more features and priority support. You can try the free version first and upgrade whenever you’re ready.
Do you offer refunds?
Yes — every premium license includes a 30-day money-back guarantee. If a plugin isn’t the right fit, contact us within 30 days for a full refund. See our Refund & Return Policy for details.
How do I get support?
Visit our Support page and submit a request. We handle pre-sales questions, suggestions, and technical support Monday through Friday, 9am–6pm PST, and typically reply within one business day.
Can I save when buying more than one plugin?
Yes — use coupon code GPFAMILY at checkout to save 15% on any of our plugins.
